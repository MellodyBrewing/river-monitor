How Safety Is Assessed
Current uses real-time USGS stream gauge data to evaluate wading safety. Two factors are combined: flow rate (CFS) and gauge height (feet). The worst of the two determines the overall rating.

Important Notes
Thresholds vary by river size and character. Always consult current conditions and local forecasts before wading. These are general guidelines — never wade in flood conditions regardless of gauge readings.

Data sourced from USGS Water Resources via the National Water Information System. Updates every 15 minutes at source.
