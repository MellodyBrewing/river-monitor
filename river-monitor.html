How Safety Is Assessed
Current uses real-time USGS stream gauge data to evaluate wading safety. Two factors are considered: flow rate (CFS) — cubic feet per second — and gauge height (feet). These are combined into a simple safety rating.

StatusCFS RangeGuidance
✓ Safe< 300 CFSGood wading

Note: Thresholds vary by river size and character. Always consult current conditions, weather forecasts, and your own skill level. These are general guidelines only — never wade in flood conditions.

Data sourced from USGS Water Resources via the National Water Information System (waterservices.usgs.gov). Updates every 15 minutes at source.
